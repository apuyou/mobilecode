# Privacy Policy

MobileCode does not collect any tracking or analytics data. All interactions happen directly between the app and your own OpenCode server.

The only exception is [Expo Updates](https://docs.expo.dev/versions/latest/sdk/updates/), which communicates with Expo servers to deliver over-the-air updates. This service may collect basic analytics such as update download status and app version information. For details, refer to [Expo's privacy policy](https://expo.dev/privacy).
