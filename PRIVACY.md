# Privacy Policy

All code and chat sessions stay between you and your own OpenCode server. MobileCode uses the following third-party services:

- [Expo Updates](https://docs.expo.dev/versions/latest/sdk/updates/) - delivers over-the-air updates. May collect update download status and app version. See [Expo's privacy policy](https://expo.dev/privacy).

- [Sentry](https://sentry.io/) - crash reporting and session tracking. Collects error information including stack traces, device info, app version, and session health metrics. See [Sentry's privacy policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/).
